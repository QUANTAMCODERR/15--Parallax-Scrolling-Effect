The soil preparation process advanced with a carefully planned layering technique, designed to enhance the soil's fertility and structure, providing an ideal environment for plant growth:

  • Rice Husk: First, rice husk was mixed with the soil to improve its texture and aeration. This essential step ensures that the soil remains well-drained, providing roots with the necessary oxygen for healthy growth. ğŸŒ¾
  • Gandul Khat: Next, gandul khat (a nutrient-rich vermicompost made from earthworm castings) was added to the soil mixture. Gandul khat is rich in organic matter, providing essential nutrients that enhance soil fertility and support plant health by fostering beneficial microbial activity. ğŸª±
  • Coconut Shells: Finally, coconut shells were incorporated into the soil. These shells not only help to improve the soilâ€™s moisture retention, which is critical for plant hydration, but also add organic material that contributes to the long-term structure and health of the soil. ğŸ¥¥

Once all of these layers were added, the soil was thoroughly watered to settle the materials and create a balanced environment conducive to plant growth. This process was carried out meticulously to ensure optimal results. ğŸ’§
